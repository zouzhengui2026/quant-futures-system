# Yinfu Trading Principles

## Core Philosophy

This system uses the principles of observation, timing, structural opportunity, disciplined action and continuous refinement.

## Eight Principles

### 1. 观 (Observation)

Understand market structure before trading.

Observe:

- Trend
- Volatility
- Liquidity
- Funding rate
- Open interest
- Leverage conditions
- Market positioning

Rule:

No observation, no trade.

### 2. 时 (Timing)

The same signal has different meanings at different market stages.

Evaluate:

- Trend emergence
- Trend expansion
- Trend maturity
- Crowding
- Exhaustion
- Reversal

### 3. 机 (Opportunity)

Seek structural turning points and high-quality opportunities.

Examples:

- Breakouts
- Liquidation events
- Volatility expansion
- Crowded positioning reversal

### 4. 盗 (Alpha Source)

Every trade must explain where the profit comes from.

Examples:

- Trend continuation
- Relative strength
- Funding imbalance
- Liquidation imbalance
- Volatility expansion

### 5. 气 (Market Flow)

Understand the invisible forces driving price.

Monitor:

- Capital flow
- Leverage flow
- Liquidity flow
- Position flow

### 6. 动 (Action)

Actions must be graded.

Possible actions:

- Observe
- No trade
- Probe
- Standard entry
- Aggressive entry
- Pyramid
- Reduce
- Exit

### 7. 克 (Counter Force)

Every alpha must have a corresponding failure condition.

Examples:

- Trend exhaustion
- Liquidity collapse
- Excessive leverage
- Regime change

### 8. 执 (Discipline)

Rules cannot be bypassed.

All orders must pass:

- Margin checks
- Liquidation risk checks
- Portfolio risk checks
- Execution controls

## Trading Decision Flow

```text
观 → 时 → 机 → 盗 → 气 → 动 → 克 → 执
```
